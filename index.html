Instructions

  • 1. Press the play button to take you to the game.
  • 2. To start the game start typing the word which is highlighted in the box above.
  • 3. To move onto the next word hit space-bar after you press the space-bar the letters that you typed will then be evaluated against the word you are currently on. Remember it has to match exactly!
  • 4. If you made a mistake or want to restart the game click the restart button to the right of the typing box, this will restart everything back to the beginning.
  • 5. (Optional) When you reach the end of the game you can then share your results by clicking one of the social media icons.
